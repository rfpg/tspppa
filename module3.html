Exponent Properties

Exponent properties are fundamental concepts in mathematics that allow us to simplify and manipulate expressions involving exponents. Exponents, also known as powers or indices, are used to represent the repeated multiplication of a number by itself. These properties provide a set of rules and relationships that govern the behavior of exponents, making calculations and problem-solving more efficient and manageable.
